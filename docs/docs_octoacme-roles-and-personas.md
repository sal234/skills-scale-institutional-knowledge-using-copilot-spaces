## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Facilitates Agile ceremonies, removes impediments, and drives continuous process improvement. Coordinates between PM, developers, QA, and stakeholders to optimize team health and velocity.

### Responsibilities
- Run daily standups, sprint planning, reviews, and retrospectives.
- Identify and remove blockers for the team.
- Coach on process and agile best practices.
- Shield the team from external disruptions.

### Goals
- Optimize delivery flow and team morale.
- Continuously improve team practices.

### Typical Communication
- Leads team ceremonies
- Escalates unresolved blockers to PM or Product Lead

### Interaction with Existing Roles
- Partners with PM for schedule and risk
- Supports developers/QA on process challenges
- Communicates impediments to stakeholders

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
Designs user flows, wireframes, and prototypes. Advocates for end-user needs and usability best practices.

### Responsibilities
- Create and validate wireframes, personas, journeys.
- Collaborate with Product Manager on requirements and acceptance criteria.
- Review designs with developers and QA.

### Goals
- Deliver interfaces that maximize usability and value.
- Reduce rework by front-loading UX research and validation.

### Typical Communication
- Shares designs in sprint or milestone planning.
- Reviews implementation with developers.

### Interaction with Existing Roles
- Works closely with Product Manager and developers.
- Incorporates feedback from Stakeholders.

## Stakeholder (Customer/End-user Representative)

### Role Summary
Represents the voice of the customer or end user during planning and reviews.

### Responsibilities
- Provide requirements, feedback, and acceptance input.
- Attend demos and review releases.
- Escalate needs or concerns to the Product Manager.

### Goals
- Ensure delivered functionality aligns with user needs.
- Build trust through regular feedback.

### Typical Communication
- Present at reviews and project updates.
- Ad hoc clarifications.

### Interaction with Existing Roles
- Collaborates with Product Manager and Project Manager.

---

## Technical Architect

### Role Summary
Guides overall technical direction, maintains alignment with strategy and standards, and supports complex design.

### Responsibilities
- Provide technical guidance and direction.
- Review technical risks or tradeoffs.
- Mentor developers and consult on feasibility.

### Goals
- Ensure scalable and maintainable solutions.
- Reduce technical debt and minimize long-term risk.

### Typical Communication
- Participates in technical reviews and planning.
- Available for design escalations.

### Interaction with Existing Roles
- Consults with developers, Product Manager, and Project Manager.
- Advises on architecture implications.
