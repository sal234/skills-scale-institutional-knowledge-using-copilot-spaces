# Personas & Roles Expansion Checklist

Use this checklist when initiating or reviewing a project to ensure clarity, reduce role ambiguity, and surface accountability.

## Checklist

- [ ] All core roles are identified (PM, Product Manager, Developers, QA)
- [ ] Scrum Master responsibilities covered (may be combined with another role in small teams)
- [ ] UX Designer or end-user experience lead is assigned for significant UI/UX changes
- [ ] Stakeholder voice (e.g., customer, end-user) is identified and engaged
- [ ] Technical Architect (or equivalent) is involved for significant technical decisions
- [ ] Roles and responsibilities documented and shared with project team
- [ ] Each role has clear deliverables and reporting cadence

## Guidance

- Review this checklist at project kickoff and at each major planning milestone.
- Use as a prompt for team agreements and onboarding.
- Consult the expanded personas document for details on responsibilities and interactions.
